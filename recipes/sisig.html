Pork Sisig

Description

This dish is a popular Filipino dish, particularly in Pampanga, made from parts of a pig, usually the face and ears, and sometimes, belly, along with chicken liver.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg pork belly or face
  • 2 onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1-2 green chillies
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp calamansi or lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mayonaise
  • Salt and pepper
  • egg(optional)

Steps

  1. Boil & grill/fry pork until cooked and crispy..
  2. Chop pork into small pieces.
  3. In a pan, saut√© garlic and onion in a bit of oil.
  4. Add chopped pork, soy sauce, calamansi, and chilies. Stir well.
  5. Mix in mayonnaise, season with salt & pepper.
  6. Optional: Crack an egg on top before serving on a sizzling plate.
