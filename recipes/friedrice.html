Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a quick and tasty dish made from leftover rice stir-fried with garlic, vegetables, egg, and sometimes meat.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (day-old)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup frozen vegetables (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© garlic until golden.
  2. Push garlic to the side, scramble the egg in the same pan.
  3. Add rice, break apart clumps, and mix well.
  4. Add soy sauce, green onions, and veggies (if using).
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Stir-fry until heated through.
