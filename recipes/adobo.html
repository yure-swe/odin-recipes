Adobo

Description

Adobo is a famous Filipino dish made by simmering meat in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and bay leaves. It’s savory, slightly tangy, and delicious with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg pork (or chicken)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 1 onion (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine meat, soy sauce, garlic, and bay leaf in a pot. Marinate for 30 mins.
  2. Add vinegar, water, and pepper. Bring to a boil without stirring.
  3. Once boiling, lower heat and simmer for 30–40 mins until tender.
  4. Optional: Fry the meat slightly for added flavor before serving.
